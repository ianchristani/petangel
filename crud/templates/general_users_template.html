About us

It is not everyone, but anyone who had their pet lost knows how difficult those moments were. Especially when we think that our little one wants to return home and doesn't know how, it is cold, hungry and in danger. Regarding this, I created this website, which aims to bring together the dots (those ones who lost their pet and the ones who found a lost pet). The site has an internal search system that will tell you the possible animals found and, who knows, maybe one of them is yours. This app means a lot in such times like that and with your help, it can be helpful.
